Are toilets and cisterns in good condition? Test flush.

Turn on the shower and taps to make sure there is good pressure and hot water.

Check for cracks in sinks, tubs and floor.

Check caulking around sinks and bathtub.

Check taps and other plumbing for leaks.

Is there enough storage space to meet your need?

Is there adequate ventilation? Check for any signs of mould.

If there is a fan, does it vent to the outside?
